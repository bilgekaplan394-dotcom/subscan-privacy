SubScan - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Summary: SubScan works offline. Your subscription and bill data is stored only on your device. We do not collect or sell your personal data.

1. Who We Are

SubScan is developed and maintained by Wueex. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at bilgekaplan394@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide

SubScan allows you to enter subscription and bill information such as name, price, billing date, and notes. This data is stored only on your device using Android's local database (Room/SQLite). It is never uploaded to our servers.

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect the following data to serve relevant ads:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • Coarse location (country level)

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Premium users who have disabled ads are not subject to AdMob data collection.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Subscription/bill data: Used only to display your information within the app. Never shared or uploaded.
  • AdMob data: Used by Google to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • Notifications: We use local notifications (AlarmManager) to remind you of upcoming payments. No data is sent to external servers for this purpose.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Google AdMob: For ad serving purposes (free users only)
  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases (premium subscription)

5. Data Storage and Security

All subscription and bill data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. Android's Auto Backup feature may back up your app data to your Google Drive account — this is controlled by your Android device settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: All your data is visible within the app.
  • Delete your data: You can delete individual subscriptions/bills within the app, or uninstall the app to remove all data.
  • Opt out of personalized ads: You can reset or limit your Advertising ID in your Android device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

SubScan is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to access, rectify, and erase your personal data. Since we store no personal data on our servers, you can exercise your right to erasure by simply deleting the app.

AdMob's use of data is governed by Google's consent framework. You may be shown a consent dialog when first using the app.
